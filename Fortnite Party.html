Rules

We have gone National, add all your friends and play Fortnite on this lobby but there are some rules for both voice chat and the normal lobby chat:

  1. Respect: Treat others with kindness and consideration, even in moments of disagreement or conflict.
  2. Constructive Criticism: If you have feedback or criticism, express it in a constructive and respectful manner, focusing on the issue at hand rather than attacking the person.
  3. Empathy: Consider how your words might affect others before speaking or writing them. Empathize with their perspective and feelings.
  4. Open-Mindedness: Be open to different opinions and perspectives. Engage in dialogue rather than shutting down or dismissing others' viewpoints.
  5. Avoid Personal Attacks: Refrain from insulting or belittling others based on their opinions, beliefs, or characteristics.
  6. Think Before Speaking: Take a moment to think about the potential impact of your words before saying them. Consider whether they contribute positively to the conversation.
  7. Listen: Practice active listening and strive to understand others' viewpoints before responding. This can help prevent misunderstandings and promote respectful communication.
  8. Focus on Solutions: Instead of dwelling on problems or resorting to negativity, focus on finding solutions and common ground.
  9. Take Responsibility: If you make a mistake or say something hurtful, apologize and take responsibility for your words.
  10. Set Boundaries: If someone else engages in searing or trash talk, calmly assert your boundaries and request respectful communication. ALSO, add all your friends and have fun!
